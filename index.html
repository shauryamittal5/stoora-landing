FAQ

Sawaal? Jawaab.

When does Stoora launch?

We're onboarding the first 500 creators from the waitlist in small batches. Join now and we'll message you on WhatsApp when it's your turn to build.

What can I sell on Stoora?

Everything, in one store: physical products (with COD), digital downloads, courses, and bookable services. You don't need separate tools for each.

Do I need a website or any tech skills?

No. If you can post on Instagram, you can run a Stoora store. The AI builds everything; you just review and approve — from your phone.

How do payments and invoices work?

Customers pay by UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm) or choose cash on delivery. Money settles to your account, and a GST-compliant invoice is generated and sent automatically with every order.

Is my Instagram account safe?

Yes. Stoora only reads your public posts to build your store — we never post, message, or act on your account.

Which languages does my store support?

English and Hindi at launch, with major regional languages rolling out right after — your store, product pages and WhatsApp messages included.
